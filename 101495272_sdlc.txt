Importance Of Learning DevOps in SDLC

- DevOps bridges the gap between development and operations for faster, smoother delivery.
- It promotes automation, reducing manual errors and speeding up deployment.
- Continuous integration and delivery (CI/CD) improve code quality and reliability.
- Collaboration across teams leads to better communication and fewer bottlenecks.
- Monitoring and feedback loops ensure rapid response to issues and user needs.
- Learning DevOps empowers developers to build scalable, maintainable, and resilient systems
