Our DevOps Management course provides an in-depth look at DevOps concepts, practices, and their practical applications in the industry.
It focuses on uniting development and operations to improve teamwork, efficiency, and automation throughout software delivery.
Students will explore the DevOps lifecycle, continuous integration (CI), continuous delivery (CD), deployment pipelines, and ways to measure DevOps success.
The course also highlights best practices for managing version control, handling dependencies, and streamlining data flow to support reliable and scalable deployments.
